# Brique Pro – Business Profit & Operations Management System

## Overview

Brique Pro is a business management platform designed to help entrepreneurs, small businesses, and independent sellers track profitability, inventory, expenses, and business performance through a centralized dashboard.

The platform was created to simplify business operations and provide real-time visibility into financial results, enabling users to make informed and data-driven decisions.

---

## Problem

Many small business owners struggle to accurately track:

- Product costs
- Sales revenue
- Operating expenses
- Inventory levels
- Profit margins
- Business performance

As a result, important decisions are often made without reliable financial information.

Brick Pro was developed to solve this challenge by providing a simple and intuitive business management solution.

---

## Solution

Brick Pro centralizes business operations in a single platform, allowing users to:

- Register products and inventory
- Record purchases and sales
- Track expenses
- Monitor profit margins
- Analyze financial performance
- Visualize business metrics through dashboards
